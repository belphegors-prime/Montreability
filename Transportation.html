Transportation

Montréal’s Société de transport offers paratransit transportation throughout the city. Accessible buses vary by time, please consult the bus schedule for further information.
Please refer to the link below for additional information.

www.stm.info/en/paratransit

Paratransit bus ramps available with the following lines:

55 Saint Laurent
(North bound and South bound)


24 Sherbrooke
( East bound and West bound )


144 Des Pins
(East bound and West bound)


150 Rene Levesque
( West bound and East bound)
